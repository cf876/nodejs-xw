Recycling Guide

Follow our simple step-by-step guide to ensure your waste is properly sorted and recycled, maximizing its value and environmental benefit.

4 Simple Steps to Recycle With Us

1

Sort Your Waste

Separate your waste into different categories (paper, plastic, metal, glass, e-waste) using our color-coded bins.

2

Book a Pickup

Schedule a convenient pickup time through our website or phone, or drop off your recyclables at our nearest center.

3

We Collect & Process

Our team will collect your recyclables and transport them to our state-of-the-art processing facility for sorting and recycling.

4

Get Rewarded

Receive competitive compensation for your recyclable materials, or enjoy tax benefits for commercial recycling programs.

Recyclable & Non-Recyclable Materials

Material Type Recyclable Notes
Paper & Cardboard Yes Remove food contamination & non-paper attachments
Plastic Bottles & Containers Yes Rinse clean and remove caps (caps are recyclable separately)
Aluminum & Steel Cans Yes Rinse clean and crush to save space
Glass Jars & Bottles Yes Rinse clean and sort by color (clear, green, brown)
Food Waste No (compostable only) Can be composted but not recycled with standard materials
Plastic Bags & Films No (special collection only) Drop off at designated retail collection points only
